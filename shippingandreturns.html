Shipping and Returns Policy

Shipping

We offer standard shipping within the United States. Shipping times may vary depending on your location.

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will initiate a refund to your original method of payment.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item, please contact us for further instructions.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Shipping and Returns Policy, please contact us.
